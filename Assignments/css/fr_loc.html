About Fremont City

  1. It was incorporated on January 23, 1956, from the merger of five smaller communities:
    1. Centerville
    2. Niles
    3. Irvington
    4. Mission San Josh
    5. Warm springs
  2. Located in the southeast section of the San Francisco Bay Area in the East Bay region primarily
  3. Fremont had a population of around 220,000.
